Typography with Food

Food,substance consisting of organic matter, typically of animal or vegetable origin, and containing carbon, hydrogen, oxygen, and nitrogen and furnishing energy.
The body requires food to sustain its vital processes, to provide energy for muscular work, and to furnish materials for growth, repair, and the replacement of tissues.
Carbohydrates, fats, proteins, vitamins, minerals, and water are the principal nutrients found in food.

Carbohydrates and fats are the chief sources of energy

Proteins are essential for the growth and repair of body tissues.
